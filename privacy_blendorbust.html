Privacy Policy

Blend or Bust Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Blend or Bust mobile app. Based on the current app implementation, Blend or Bust is designed as a local party game and does not collect, sell, or share personal data for advertising or tracking purposes.

What Blend or Bust does not collect

  • No user accounts or profiles are required.
  • No analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies are used.
  • No gameplay data is sent to a backend server operated for the app.
  • No in-app purchases, subscriptions, or marketing databases are used.

Data used locally on your device

Based on the current app code, Blend or Bust stores a small amount of data locally on the device so the app can work as expected. This includes:

  • selected language
  • sound on or off
  • vibration on or off
  • whether the info screen has already been shown
  • custom deck names and custom deck word lists you choose to save

Player names and active round information are handled in app memory for the current session. Based on the current implementation, that session data is not written to the app's persistent local storage.

Support email

If you contact support at coding98jones@gmail.com, your email address and any information you include in your message will be used only to respond to your request and manage support.

Email is handled by your email provider and the recipient's email provider, outside the app itself.

Your choices

You can delete saved custom decks from within the app. You can also stop using the app at any time. Because app data is stored locally on your device, removing the app from the device will generally remove that locally stored app data as part of the app removal process.

For privacy questions, contact coding98jones@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

If Blend or Bust changes how it handles data in a future version, this Privacy Policy should be updated before or at the time the new data practice is introduced.
