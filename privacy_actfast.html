Privacy Policy

Act Fast Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Act Fast mobile app. Act Fast is designed as a local party game. Based on the current app implementation, the app does not collect, sell, or share personal data for advertising or tracking purposes.

What Act Fast does not collect

  • No user accounts or profiles are required.
  • No analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies are used.
  • No player data is sent to a backend server operated for the app.
  • No in-app purchases, subscriptions, or marketing databases are used.

Data used locally on your device

During gameplay, players may enter names and custom words. Based on the current app code, that gameplay data is handled locally in the running app session and is not written to the app's persistent storage for long-term retention.

The app does store a small amount of non-personal preference data locally on the device so the app can work as expected. This includes:

  • selected language
  • sound on or off
  • vibration on or off
  • whether the informational intro screen has already been shown

Support email

If you contact support at coding98jones@gmail.com, your email address and any information you include in your message will be used only to respond to your request and manage support.

Email is handled by your email provider and the recipient's email provider, outside the app itself.

Your choices

You can stop using the app at any time. Because app preferences are stored locally on your device, removing the app from the device will generally remove that locally stored app data as part of the app removal process.

For privacy questions, contact coding98jones@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

If Act Fast changes how it handles data in a future version, this Privacy Policy should be updated before or at the time the new data practice is introduced.
