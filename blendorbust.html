Blend or Bust support

Blend or Bust is a local pass-and-play imposter word game. Most players see the same secret word, one or more imposters bluff, and the group tries to spot who does not belong. If you need help with setup, custom decks, language, or round flow, this is the public support page for the app.

How to get help

For support, contact coding98jones@gmail.com.

To make troubleshooting faster, include your device model, OS version, app version, and a short note about whether the issue happened during player setup, deck editing, private reveal, or the final result flow.

  1. Explain the issue in one or two sentences.
  2. List the steps that led to it.
  3. Say whether you were using English or German.
  4. Mention whether custom decks or hint assistance were involved.

Quick facts

  • No account is required to play.
  • The game is designed for local one-device play.
  • English and German are supported in the app.
  • Saved custom decks stay on the device unless you delete them.

Common questions

Does Blend or Bust need an internet connection? Normal gameplay is designed to run locally on the device. Only viewing this support site or sending support email uses your internet connection.
Are player names or custom decks synced online? No online sync is built into the current app. Player names are held for the current session, while saved custom decks are kept locally on the device.
Can I switch the app language? Yes. The app currently supports English and German. The chosen language is saved locally so future launches reuse it.
What happens if a round is interrupted? If you leave a round, it can be started again. The app is built for quick restart between rounds rather than long recovery flows.
