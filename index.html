Act Fast support

Act Fast is a local party game for charades-style rounds with teams, words, sound, and quick handoffs. If something is unclear or not working as expected, this page is the public support contact for the app.

How to get help

For support, contact coding98jones@gmail.com.

To make troubleshooting faster, include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of what happened.

  1. Explain the issue in one or two sentences.
  2. List the steps that led to it.
  3. Say whether sound, vibration, or language settings were changed.

Quick facts

  • No account is required to play.
  • The game is designed to work locally on the device.
  • The privacy policy for the app is available below.

Common questions

Does Act Fast need an internet connection? The gameplay is designed to run locally on the device. If you are reviewing the support or privacy pages online, that of course uses your browser connection.
Why are sound or vibration not working? Check the in-app settings first. Also verify that your device is not muted and that vibration is enabled at the system level.
Do I need to sign in or create a profile? No. Act Fast does not require user accounts for normal use.
